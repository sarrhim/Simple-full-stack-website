Personal statement

Belgium-born environmental engineer who has been living in Southeast Asia for more than a decade after working on almost every continent. He arrived in Cambodia in 2002 to map minefields and assist with demining. He then developed surveillance systems to locate and assist mine and ERW casualties in Cambodia.
